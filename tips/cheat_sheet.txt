=======================================================
REMEMBER YOU ARE SIGNING FOR THE PERSON IN FRONT OF YOU
=======================================================
POSITION YOUR HANDS AT BODY HEIGHT
=======================================================

____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
1) A, E, I, O, U ==> Each Vowel corresponds to each finger on your hand, starting with 'A' at the thumb and 'U' at the pinky
____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
2) L, M, N, R, V ==> These letters are signed in front of your palm
____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
3) X, W ==> For 'W' interlace all of your fingers, for 'X' only cross your index fingers
____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
4) C, D, K, P, Q ==> These letters are meant to look like the way they are written
____________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
